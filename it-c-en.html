Consulting


For a strategic development, our consulting services aim to optimize all technological processes and business flows in the relationship with our customers. We provide best practices and support our customers in the process of digital transformation with the latest technologies in the field. Our consultants advise you in identifying and selecting the right solutions for your business, which are the foundation for development and provide support in designing the right business processes, optimizing financial and time resources.

Analyze how current systems work and meet your business needs, as well as identify inefficient processes and causes of performance delays.

Implementation consulting

Technological modernization

Our team of consultants will help you identify the most appropriate technology and integration approach for your environment, minimizing the inconvenience and risks of transformation and implementation.

IMPLEMENTATION


Implementation is especially important for the success of a project and contributes decisively to optimizing business activity. The team of New Business Dimensions professionals brings to projects the expertise obtained through multiple certifications and a vast experience of over 10 years of activity. We constantly invest in preparing our human resources so that they are always in step with the latest in business and technology. The implementation methodology includes predefined scenarios and flows according to the specifics and needs of each client.

Business Analysis

The analysis services aim to identify the appropriate technical solutions and to achieve the specific objectives of the business, by:

  • Synthesizing the business profile and the technologies used;
  • Identify the needs and translate them into requirements and solutions, so that the implementation team has a well-defined set of specifications, after which they can make a plan and the implementation can proceed smoothly;
  • Support throughout the implementation period to cover all requirements;

Management de proiect

The implementation of an IT project cannot be done without a professional project management from both the implementer and the client. The benefits you can get from using our project management services are:

  • Project overview;
  • Effective risk management;
  • Availability and flexibility in project implementation;
  • Advanced team experience and coordination of its members;

Implementation
